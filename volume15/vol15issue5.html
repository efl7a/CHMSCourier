Dear families,

Please help me in welcoming a new staff member to the CHMS family. For several years I have been advocating for CHMS to receive an additional assistant principal due to our ever-growing size. I have been informed this week that Mr. Buku Guzeh will be joining our team on Monday as a new AP! Mr. Guzeh has been serving as an AP at EE Waddell Language Academy for the past several years and prior to that he taught both French and Elementary school. We are excited to add him to the team.

We also are continuing to work on locating a permanent Math I teacher to fill the vacancy created when Mr. Runda resigned the first day of school. Currently, my math interventionist is teaching these classes and is doing a fantastic job. As soon as we have a permanent plan we will communicate to families.

Don't forget to mark your calendars for Curriculum Night this week. Our 6th grade event is Tuesday from 6pm - 7:45pm and the 7th/8th grade event is Thursday at the same time. You will walk through your child's schedule and we do start promptly at 6pm. Parking will be tight so feel free to use the bus parking lot as well.

Thank you for your ongoing support of our school, staff and students!
