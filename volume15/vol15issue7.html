A Message from Principal Brooks

Dear families,

We have a lot going on next week here at CHMS. Spirit week begins on Monday with Meme Monday. Tuesday will be Pink Out day with our Pink Out football game occurring that afternoon. Wednesday will be Workout Wednesday, Thursday is Once Upon a Time (throwback) and Friday is Team Spirit day. We will have our annual In It to Win It games on Friday which is always a lot of fun for the staff and students! Also, donâ€™t forget that Wednesday is an Early Release day for students. The school day will end at 1:15pm so that teachers can participate in professional development in the afternoon.

Finally, parents I need your help in speaking with students about how to respond if they perceive there to be any sort of threat to our school. This week we had a non-credible threat, a cartoon drawing that several kids added silly comments and drawings to, which quickly became a concern as a result of students turning to snapchat. If your child comes home with any sort of concern, please email me directly upon hearing the concern. Calling neighbors and turning to social media creates undue hysteria. I promise you that any and all concerns will be investigated. Please allow us to do our jobs in this area without the increased panic brought about by social media. Thank you so much!
