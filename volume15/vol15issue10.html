A Message from Principal Brooks

Dear families,

With the Thanksgiving holiday fast approaching I wanted to take a moment and tell each and every one of you how grateful I am to work with your children. We have an amazing family here at Community House and I am blessed to be a part of it. A special shout out to Ms. McAfee and our PTO, along with all of our volunteers, who have worked tirelessly to put together Thanksgiving baskets for many of our families. Also, a shout out to Meals4America, led by Phil Raleigh, who are also providing wonderful hot meals for many of our families. Together...we can make a difference in the community.

I hope everyone has a happy Thanksgiving next week. School will be closed Wednesday through Friday. Enjoy your family and friends!!

Donate to CHMS PTO

The PTO Capital Campaign is running 50% below goal. Help us close the gap by donating $10 to the CHMS Capital Campaign on Giving Tuesday, December 3, 2019.

Did you know that CMS does not pay all the costs for running a school the size of Community House? Our PTO helps fill these gaps by:

  • funding science and technology supplies for the classrooms
  • funding school improvements such as the school sign, the expanded sidewalks, and landscape maintenance and improvements
  • providing agendas, Springboard books and required reading books to in-need Community House students.
  • delivering monthly teacher appreciation events and goodies
  • providing many other types of support for students, staff and families at Community House

Please support your school on Tuesday, December 3, 2019

Cookie Exchange

December 6, 2019

If you’re not familiar with the event, it’s a staff favorite! Parents donate homemade cookies or treats. We set up our “cookie boutique” in the Media Center. Then, our fabulous staff members check in, pick up a holiday shopping bag and go shopping!

This year we have approximately 150 staff members. We also give cookies to the 60 AM and PM bus drivers. Our overall cookie goal this year is 870 dozen cookies! It sounds like a lot, but we can do it with your help!

Cookies need to be dropped off by Thursday, December 5th in the PTO room.
