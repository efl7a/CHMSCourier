Dear families,

We received great news this week from the state regarding our test results from the 2018-2019 school year. I am extremely excited to tell you that CHMS has again been named an A school with high growth in both math and reading. In fact, out of the nearly 2500 public and charter schools in North Carolina, CHMS ranked 20th overall in growth. Additionally, CHMS ranked number one in the state for math growth…you read that correctly…Community House had the highest growth score in math of all NC public and charter schools!! I am also very proud of our ELA department who ranks 8th in CMS for middle school reading growth. I say it all the time…we have the best staff in CMS...and this data is just one more piece of evidence to support that! I would say to tell all your neighbors that are paying for private school but we are already overcrowded. 🙂

Thank you so much for all that you do to support our school and our students. We could not be as successful as we are without your help every day!!

Future Problem Solvers

After last year’s success at State Levels+, Future Problem Solving will continue at CHMS! FPS is a club where students are taught to think creatively about the future. This will be conducted every Thursday after school from 4.30 pm to 5.30 pm.

This year's topics include International Travel, Sleep Patterns, Gamification, and Living in Poverty.

For further details please attend the information session on September 12 at 5 pm at CHMS in the art room. Hope to see you there!
