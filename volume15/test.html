9 Teacher Workday - No School for Students

Dear families,

The first week of school is now under our belts and, aside from computer outages and broken water pipes, it has been a great success!! We apologize for the late buses but this is always expected during the first week or two as the district adjusts routes to meet the needs of unexpected ridership. Thank you for your patience!

As of right now our numbers are down a bit from what was originally enrolled. We seem to be around 1920 as opposed to 1980. We will continue to assess our No-Shows to see if there is any availability in elective courses where some of you have made requests. Often times some kids show up the day after Labor Day so we do plan to wait until next week to make changes.

Monday is a school holiday for everyone. Don't forget that our 6th grade dance is Friday, September 6th from 4:15pm - 6:00pm. Your child should bring home information about this event so ask if you have not seen it. Also, mark September 12th on your calendars if you are interested in our Navigating Middle School Social Media event. This will begin at 6pm in the school cafeteria and it is for parents only.
