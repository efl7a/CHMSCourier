A Message from Principal Brooks

Dear families,

I wanted to take a minute and welcome two new members of the CHMS family...Mr. Jon Capen and Ms. Makeda Johnson. Mr. Capen joined us a few weeks ago following Mr. Ferri's retirement. He is teaching 7th grade science and knows many of you as he lives in our community. Ms. Johnson has come on board to fill in for Ms. Jacobs who recently gave birth to a healthy baby boy, Finnegan! Ms. Johnson joins us from University Park Creative Arts Academy where she served as a literacy coach. We are so grateful to have both of them on our team

Our registration process for next year will be starting up at the end of this month. We will host our rising 6th grade families the week of March 24th and our in-house 7/8 grade students on the 31st. Be on the lookout soon for more information. Also, applications for NJHS went home this week. If your child is applying please make sure that he/she takes the application process seriously as these are scored on a rubric. They also need to be submitted on time in order to be considered for membership. If you have any questions, please reach out to Ms. Valerie Johnson.

March 13 is the next Teacher Luncheon. Sign up to bring food or help serve. All help is greatly appreciated!

Odyssey of the Mind Team takes 1st at Central Region Tournament

Taran Karthik Bellam, Sneha Kethan, and Nethra Gopalakrishnan, 6th graders at CHMS, were part of a team that took home 1st place in their division at the Central Region Tournament. The team now advances to the State Tournament on March 21, 2020.

Their team also won the prestigious Ranatra Fusca Award for their outstanding out of the box thinking and creative style. This award was only given to 2 out of 80 teams competing.

Congratulations Taran, Sneha, and Nethra! Good Luck at States!
