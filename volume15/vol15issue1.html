Message from the Principal

Dear families,


Welcome back to the 2019 – 2020 school year! I am very excited to begin my 12th year as the principal of our amazing school. I hope everyone had a restful summer but that you are ready to send your kids back to us. Open Houses will be next Friday, August 23rd from 8:30am – 4:30pm. The back to school letter contained specific times for 6th grade teams and drop-in hours for upperclassmen. Please adhere to your times as our parking lot has limited space. Our current enrollment as of today is 1956!! Don’t worry…we will take great care of each and every one of your children.


For the first day of school on August 26th your child will only need to bring paper and writing utensils. Teachers will let you know about additional supplies so that you do not purchase unnecessary items. Pay4schoolstuff.com has links for parents to purchase PE wear, school agendas (required fees) and donate to the Capital Campaign (our only fundraiser). This information is also in the back to school letter which was mailed this week. If you did not receive a mailer, please contact the school at 980-343-0689 or email me (jamie.brooks@cms.k12.nc.us) and we can get you what you need.


I look forward to a wonderful year and cannot wait to see everyone next Friday for Open House!!
