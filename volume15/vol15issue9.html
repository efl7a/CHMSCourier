A Message from Principal Brooks

Dear families,

I am very hopeful to see many of you on Tuesday night at 6pm in the media center for our Parent Forum on diversity and inclusion. This is something we started last year and received extremely positive feedback on the dialogue. It is our goal this year to continue our efforts by creating a safe environment to talk about how diversity impacts us and our children in the Ballantyne community. This year we hope to expand the conversations into a book study of The Hate U Give. Please consider joining us on Tuesday to learn more. Email me if you plan to attend.

Don't forget that Monday is a holiday as we recognize and honor all of our Veterans. Thank you to those who serve or have served our country!!

Finally, tickets for our Fall Musical, All Shook Up, go on sale Tuesday morning at 8:45am in the school lobby. Do not miss out on the opportunity to come experience the music of Elvis and the amazing talents of our FAB department. Shows are Friday and Saturday nights at 7pm. Tickets are $8 and will likely sell out both nights...buy early!!

Have a great week!!

What We Do

We ask that the school community come together to fund the wonderful things our administrators, faculty, and staff request to make CHMS a place to learn and thrive for every student.

We ask that you take advantage of corporate matching programs available to you.

We ask that you give what you are able.

What We Don't Do

We do not ask students and parents to go door-to-door selling items.

We don't ask that you contact friends and family requesting money.
