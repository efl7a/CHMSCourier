A Message from Principal Brooks

Dear families,

As many of you have probably seen by now our school was impacted by what I believe was a false Instagram account. Many of our kids were asked to follow the account and the person behind it began asking inappropriate questions of these boys. I cannot stress enough the importance of monitoring your children on social media. All of the kids impacted by this are smart, well-intentioned young men. Their parents are highly involved and they still became targets. It is simply not safe for our kids to attempt to boost their online popularity by accepting follows and friend requests from people they have never actually met. I know the kids want to have as many followers as possible as that is a status statement these days...but it truly can be dangerous. It wasn't that long ago that a child in our very own community went missing for a year after meeting someone online. Please engage your children in these crucial conversations about online safety. Their perceived popularity is simply not worth the risks. Thank you for all you do for our school and community. Have a great week!
