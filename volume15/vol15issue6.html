Dear families,

We have quite a few events coming up in the next few weeks that I would like to put on your radars. First, October 2 is School Picture Day. Attention 7th Grade Parents

Last week, eligible 7th-grade students received information regarding the DUKE TIP program. DUKE TIP provides 7th-grade students the opportunity to take the SAT or ACT. By participating, students can earn recognition and attend academic enrichment programs. Students who scored in the 95th percentile or above on the NC Math or Reading EOG in 5th or 6th grade are eligible.

If you are new to CMS and your child scored in the 95th percentile or above on another state or nationally normed test, your child is eligible to participate. Please contact Valerie Johnson to verify your eligibility.

Register for the AMC 8/10 Math Competitions

The AMC 8 is a math contest for middle and elementary school students meant to test their knowledge against other students in the nation. If your student loves math and wants to see how they match up with their peers, the AMC 8 is for them. The AMC 10 is meant for more advanced students as they are allowed to take the contest up through 10th grade.

Tuesday, October 1, 2019 is the last day to turn in AMC 8 & 10 money. Have your student bring $6 (cash only) to the PTO Room Tuesday morning from 8:45-9:15.
