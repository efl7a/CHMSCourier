A Message from CHMS PTO

Dear Community House Middle School Families,

Welcome back to all returning families and a special greeting to rising 6th grade parents and new families to Community House Middle School. We are looking forward to a successful 2020-2021 school year.

The role of the PTO at Community House Middle School is to support the students, parents, faculty and staff in the best way possible. With shrinking budgets, larger class sizes and increasing demands on our teachers and staff (ALL of which directly impact your child), there has never been a more important time for you, as parents, to get involved and join our PTO community. Your support and/or involvement is crucial to our success.

There are many ways to support the PTO’s initiatives at Community House Middle School. One way to support our efforts and initiatives is through volunteering. There are many opportunities throughout the school year when we need your help. Ben Franklin once said, “You tell me and I forget. You teach me and I remember. You involve me and I learn.” Even if you work full time, part time, or if you are a stay at home parent, we have a place for everyone.

Another way is to show your support is to join the PTO -- your membership DOES make a difference. Our goal is to have 100% participation from every family. To join the PTO, please visit www.PAY4SCHOOLSTUFF.com.

Lastly, you can also contribute through Community House’s capital campaign. The funds collected from our capital campaign help to provide the learning tools necessary for your child’s education. This is the MAIN fundraiser for the entire year. Your contribution, whether small or large, is greatly appreciated and needed to continue to provide things such as:

  • Technology - updates and maintenance as needed: Microsoft certification programs
  • Books and other media for Media Center
  • Professional development for our teachers
  • Agendas, school supplies and required reading for students in need
  • Support for Student Clubs
  • EOG study material
  • Health Room Supplies
  • Science Lab Supplies
  • Social Studies Software program
  • Support our sister school, Albemarle Road Middle School
  • Campus beautification

These are just a few examples of what your contributions have done in the past that help support our school and your child’s educational experience. Please consider volunteering, joining the PTO and/or making a contribution to our Capital Campaign – every little bit matters.
